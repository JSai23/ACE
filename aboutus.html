More Information About Us

What is Ace Club in detail and what does ACE Stand For?

Ace Club is the Attitude Chracter and Education Club. This club helps teach
younger children in kindergarten, first grade, and second grade about different character
values.

How do we do it?

We do this by going to the same 6 classes in each grade every month to teach a
specific value. We use activities books and other resources to be able to do this.
We also use kids who attend the club to help teach.

Why we do it?

We do this because we believe that it is very important for character valuest to be cultivated
in young children from a very young age. These values will help the children for there whole
life by making them well behaved in the long run.

How we are connected to Character?

We connect to character because we help chidlren with their character for the future.
We influence mainly the character of our future generation. This is important because
this will shape our future.

Our History

Behind us there are many times that have shaped us, formed us and made us an effective and good organization
We started back in the year of 2014 at the beggining of the annual school year. During this period we formed
the club and got the basic ides up and running. We talked to our schools administrators and other facility
so that we would be able to communicate with teachers so that we could start teaching. During this period we
we only had about 5 to 7 members whow ere all just friends. During this year we only taught one or two times.
Then in the year of 2016-2017 we decided to continue this idea from the open house of our school to get people
to joing the club. At the first meeting we had over twenty kids from 5-8th grade interested in teaching.
