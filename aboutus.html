ACE CLUB

What is Ace Club in detail and what does ACE Stand For?

Ace Club is the Attitude Chracter and Education Club. This club helps teach
younger children in kindergarten, first grade, and second grade about different character
values.

How do we do it

We do this by going to the same 6 classes in each grade every month to teach a specific value.
We use activities books and other resources to be able to do this. We also use kids who attend the
club to help teach.
