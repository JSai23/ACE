What is Ace Club in detail and what does ACE Stand For?

Ace Club is the Attitude Chracter and Education Club. This club helps teach
younger children in kindergarten, first grade, and second grade about different character
values.

How do we do it?

We do this by going to the same 6 classes in each grade every month to teach a
specific value. We use activities books and other resources to be able to do this.
We also use kids who attend the club to help teach.

Why we do it

We do this because we believe that it is very important for character valuest to be cultivated
in young children from a very young age. These values will help the children for there whole
life by making them well behaved in the long run.
